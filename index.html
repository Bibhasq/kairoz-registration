What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to artificial intelligence systems that can autonomously plan, decide, and act across complex tasks — without constant human input. Unlike traditional AI that merely responds to prompts, agentic systems pursue goals, use tools, coordinate with other agents, and adapt in real time to achieve outcomes end-to-end.
